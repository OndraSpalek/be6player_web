About the Project
The Sixth Player
on Every Team

BE6PLAYER was born from the belief that data can tell a story that you simply can't see just by watching a game. Every move, every pass, every decision leaves a trace in the numbers.

Our channel brings sports statistics, trends and fascinating insights across various leagues and sports. From data breakdowns to surprising facts — we make sports analytics understandable, engaging and accessible for every fan.

As the sixth player, we see what others overlook. And we share it with you.
